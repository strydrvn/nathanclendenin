The Work

Discover the Timeless Beauty of Culver Academies

Nathan, a 10-time Emmy Award-winning storyteller and CMA graduate ('96), has an exceptional talent for capturing the essence of the places he loves. As a former Bandsman at Culver, he brings a personal connection and deep appreciation to his panoramic photos of the Academy.

Taken in the stillness of a summer morning in 2023, his latest work reflects the tranquil beauty and historic charm of Culver Academies. Each image, bathed in the soft light of dawn, invites viewers to experience the serene landscapes and quiet moments that Nathan holds dear. His work isn't just about the visuals; it's about the stories and emotions that these places evoke.

About the Artist

A Storyteller with Deep Roots at Culver

Nathan's journey as a father and professional has shaped his keen eye for beauty and detail. As the Director of Marketing and Communication at an independent school in North Carolina, he has cultivated a deep appreciation for storytelling.

Through his agency, StoryDriven, Nathan captures moments that reveal the essence of his subjects. His time as a Bandsman at Culver Academies remains a significant influence, adding layers of nostalgia and connection to his work. Each photograph is a quiet narrative, reflecting his experiences and the enduring spirit of his alma mater.
