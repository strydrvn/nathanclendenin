About

Capturing moments that feel like your family

I'm Nathan Clendenin, a photographer based in the Triangle area. My background is in photojournalism, and I have two decades of experience capturing moments that tell a story. My wife, Rebecca, and I have four children and are often hosting an international student. We love community, camping and visiting National Parks across the U.S.

Photo sessions take place at Solterra Co-Housing Community, a beautiful natural setting with mature trees and warm evening light. Every session is relaxed, low-key, and genuinely fun for everyone, including the kids.
